Expert talk on Computers and Medicine by Dr Tavpritesh Sethi

ACM IGDTUW conducted expert talks on 'Computational Techniques for Social Good' for summer workshop cum internship 2021. Dr Tavpritesh Sethi was the expert speaker on 2nd July, 2021 from 11am to 12pm.Dr Tavpriresh talked about 'Computers and Medicines'. Dr. Tavpritesh Sethi is a physician-scientist and Associate Professor of Computational Biology at IIIT-D. Over the past two years, he was a visiting faculty member at Stanford University, School of Medicine. Trainees were very enthusiastic to gain knowledge from an esteemed professor.
