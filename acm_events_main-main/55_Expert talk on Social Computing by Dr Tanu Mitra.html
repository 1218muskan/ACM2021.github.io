Expert talk on Social Computing by Dr Tanu Mitra

ACM IGDTUW conducted an expert talk on 'Computational Techniques for Social Good' by Dr Tanu Mitra on 16th July 2021 at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop cum internship 2021 from 9am to 10am. Dr Tanu Mitra talked about 'Social Computing'. She is an assistant professor at University of Washington. From August 2017 to 2020, She was an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. She completed my PhD in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, where she was a Foley Scholar.
