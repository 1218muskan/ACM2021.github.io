Alumni Research Talk by Ms Madhura Pande

ACM IGDTUW conducted Alumni Research talk under the banner of ACM IGDTUW summer workshop cum internship 2021 on 5th July,2021 from 4pm to 5pm. She is a Data scientist at Flipkart.
