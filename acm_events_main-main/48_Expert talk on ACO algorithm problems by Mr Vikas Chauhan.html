Expert talk on ACO algorithm problems by Mr Vikas Chauhan

ACM Student Chapter IGDTUW organized its third Expert Talk on Optimization Techniques for Social Good by Mr. Vikas Chauhan under Summer Workshop cum Internship 2021 on 18th June 2021 from 4pm to 5:30pm. Questions related to the ACO algorithm were discussed and participants were allowed to ask their doubts at the end.
