Tech Week - Advaya 2.O

Advaya 2.0, the annual tech event of ACM IGDTUW was proudly announced on 6 April 2020. It consisted of a weeklong exciting sessions, full of opportunities from April 12 to April 18 There were sessions by eminent speakers, a vast range of competitions with amazing prizes! The official theme of Advaya was Stems of STEM which signified just as a stem nourishes the plant and transports nutrients, in a similar way STEM education imparts valuable contribution to the all-round development. The Official Mascot of Advaya 2.0 was The Coderina. The first five days witnessed sessions on OpenSource, Entrepreneurship for girls, Alexa, AR/VR as well as Game Development and Tips for Competitive coding with some astonishing speakers followed by a STEMathon.
