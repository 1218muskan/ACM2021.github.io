Alumni Talk by Ms Aakriti Budhraja

ACM IGDTUW invited Ms Aakriti Budhraja for an alumni research talk at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop cum Internship on 16th July 2021 from 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm. Ms. Aakriti Budhraja is an upcoming data scientist at Myntra. She has been an IGDTUW alumnus (Batch 2015, IT branch) and further pursued MS Computer Science from IIT Madras. During her college days, she was deeply associated with ACM Student Chapter, IGDTUW which remains indispensable to her growth. She also has an experience of 2 years at Microsoft as an engineer.
