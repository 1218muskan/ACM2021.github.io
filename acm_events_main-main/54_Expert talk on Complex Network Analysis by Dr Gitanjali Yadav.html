Expert talk on Complex Network Analysis by Dr Gitanjali Yadav

ACM IGDTUW conducted an expert talk on 'Computational Techniques for Social Good' by Dr. Gitanjali Yadav at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop cum Internship on 9th July 2021 from 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm. Dr Gitanjali discussed Complex Network Analysis. Currently she is a Visiting Faculty at the University of Cambridge. Dr. Gitanjali pursued her Bachelor’s and master’s degree from DU while completing her Ph.D. degree from NII, JNU, New Delhi. Trainees were inspired by her depth of knowledge.
