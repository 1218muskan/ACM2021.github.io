Expert talk on Evolutionary Computing by Dr Mamta Madan

ACM IGDTUW invited Prof. Mamta Madan as an Expert Speaker at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop cum Internship on 20th July 2021 from 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm. Dr. Mamta talked about 'Evolutionary Computation'. Dr. Mamta Madan is a professor at VIPS-Vivekananda Institute of Professional Studies. Previously she worked as a Systems Associate at NIIT Technologies. She completed her BSc in Physics from DU, MBA and MCA from Jamia Milia University and PhD in CS from Banasthali University. Her fields of interest are Cloud Computing, data mining, and AI.
