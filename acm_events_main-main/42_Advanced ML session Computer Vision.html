Advanced ML session: Computer Vision

ACM IGDTUW conducted expert talks on advanced topics of Machine Learning under the banner of ACM IGDTUW summer workshop 2021. Mr Hitkul Jangid was invited as a speaker for Computer Vision: an Advanced ML topic on 22/6/21. He discussed Augmentation & GANs Classification Segmentation, Object Detection & Siamese Networks. The session had 50+ attendees. He is currently a PhD Scholar at IIITD and works in the Precog & MIDAS research group. He is a Research Intern at Goldman Sachs and also a TCS Research Fellow. He discussed topics like Computer Visio, Transformer, GANs etc. in the session.
