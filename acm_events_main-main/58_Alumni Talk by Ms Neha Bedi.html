Alumni Talk by Ms Neha Bedi

ACM IGDTUW is pleased to invite Ms. Neha Bedi as an Alumni Speaker at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop cum Internship on 15th July 2021 from 12:00 pm to 1:00 pm.Neha has been a student of MCA batch: 2011-14, at IGDTUW (then IGIT). She completed her Masters in Dependable Software Systems from the University of British Columbia. Ms. Neha is currently working in Amazon as SDE 2, in the AWS. Previously she has worked with companies like Slalom and Thoughtfocus Technologies. During her days at IGIT, she was an active member of the ACM, Association for Computing Machinery even then.
