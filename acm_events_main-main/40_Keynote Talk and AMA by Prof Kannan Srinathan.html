Keynote Talk and AMA by Prof Kannan Srinathan

ACM IGDTUW conducted a keynote session by Prof Kannan Srinathan on 'Computational Techniques for Social Good' at 7th ACM Workshop cum internship 2021 on 1st July from 4pm to 5pm. Dr Kannan is an assistant professor at IIIT-H. He completed his Ph.D at IIT Madras. He discussed his penchant topics like cyber security to the trainees. The strength of participants was over 50 and they thoroughly enjoyed the keynote session.
