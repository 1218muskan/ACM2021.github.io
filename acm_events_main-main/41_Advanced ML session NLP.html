Advanced ML session: NLP

ACM IGDTUW invited Ms Shivnangi Singhal as a speaker on Advanced ML topics at the 7th ACM Summer Workshop and Internship on 21/6/21. She discussed topics related to NLP, Attention & Transformer BERT, Code Mixed Indian Languages etc. The session was attended by 50+ people. Ms Shivangi is currently a PhD Scholar at IIITD and works in the Precog & MIDAS research group. She is jointly advised by Prof Ponnurangam Kumarguru and Prof Rajiv Ratn Shah. She is a Research Intern at Goldman Sachs. In the past, she has done research internship at NII, Japan.
