Keynote Talk and AMA by Prof Ponnurangam Kumaraguru

A keynote talk and AMA session was held by Prof Ponnurangam Kumarguru on 14 June,2021 from 12pm to 1pm. It was the first keynote talk under the banner of ACM IGDTUW summer workshop cum internship 2021. Prof. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru is a Professor at IIIT-Hyderabad and Head of Precog Research Lab. PK sir is also a TEDx speaker and an ACM Distinguished & ACM India Eminent Speaker. Over 60+ enthusiasts attended this insightful session and got to learn from the experiences of Prof Ponnurangam.
