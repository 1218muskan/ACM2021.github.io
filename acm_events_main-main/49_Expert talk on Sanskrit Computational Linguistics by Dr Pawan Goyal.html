Expert talk on Sanskrit Computational Linguistics by Dr Pawan Goyal

ACM IGDTUW conducted expert talks on 'Computational Techniques for Social Good' under the banner of ACM IGDTUW summer workshop cum internship 2021. Dr. Pawan Goyal, an Associate Professor at IIT Kharagpur led a session on 'Sanskrit Computational Linguistics'. He is a recipient of the esteemed Google India AI/ML Research award,2020. He was a postdoctoral researcher at INRIA. The session was lauded by all trainees.
